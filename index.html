About Me

Hi! I'm Long Le. I graduated with a dual degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from UMass Amherst. I'm interested in many areas of AI. I think it'd be cool if we can build AI that is as smart as the robot Baymax from Disney's Big Hero 6 movie. I'm orginally from Vietnam. I like cooking, playing chess and swimming in my free time.

I join Google as a software engineer in February 2022. During college, I've the opportunities to do research with Professor Katia Sycara from the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, and Professor Yair Zick and Professor Yao Li from UMass Amherst. I've also spent a summer at Facebook (now known as Meta).
